What is Hour of Code?
 The Hour of Code is a global movement
            that covers several million
            students and pupils from more than 180 countries.
            Everyone can become an organizer or a participant. 
			Hour of Code's mission is to introduce participants to
            programming in simple and
            fascinating way and facilitate love for STEM disciplines.
            Fill out the form on the website and you can
            take a 45-minute individual short lesson.
            Materials of the Programming Week event will be availble 
			later.
